Konnichiwa is a deflationary token designed to become more scarce over time.

Watch the amount of Konnichiwa supply decrease automatically as each buy and sell transaction burns a 1% fee from on chain transaction that happens on the Konnichiwa ecosystem.

There is a 10% tax fee on every buy and sell and is broken down into the following:
5% of the tax goes to the liquidity pool
4% goes to support marketing efforts
1% Auto burn feature: Thus, coins are burned with each on chain transaction.

There is an antiwhale system so no person can hold more than 4% of the supply and you can not sell more than 4% of the supply in one transaction.
