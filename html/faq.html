Frequently Asked Questions

What makes Konnichiwa different from the other meme coins?

We think our strengths are our transparency & our desire to push this project to maximum heights. Also as a $KONN holder, you are basically paid to hold the token.

When will you burn more tokens?

We have already burned 50% of the supply at launch and also there will be a constant burn from the transaction taxes so the supply will gradually go down.

Is $KONN a deflationary meme token?

The short answer is yes! All the burned tokens have been sent to a dead wallet (nobody has access to this wallet). Then this dead wallet also gets proportional transactions rewards too. The burn happens on EVERY TRANSACTION and accelerates as the wallet gets bigger & forever!

How do I see my $KONN tokens in Metamask ?

1. Click on the "ADD TOKEN" button
2. Select "Custom Token" tab
3. Paste the contract address from the first step to the "Token Address" field: INSERT TOKEN ADDRESS
4. Click the "NEXT" button
5. Click the "ADD TOKENS" button
6. You should see them now.
